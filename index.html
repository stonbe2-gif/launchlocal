FAQ

Clear terms prevent bad sales calls.

Most buyers worry about ownership, cancellation, and hidden fees. Put the answers on the page.

Is monthly required?

No. Most clients can pay for the build upfront. Monthly care is optional and only makes sense if you want us to host, monitor, support, and edit the site.

Do I own the domain?

Yes. Your domain should stay in your name. We can help connect it and manage the technical setup.

What happens if I cancel?

After the 6-month minimum, you can cancel the monthly plan. If you want the site files transferred, we quote that clearly before launch.

What counts as a small edit?

Text changes, new photos, hours, service updates, phone numbers, seasonal banners, and simple page updates. New page designs or major redesigns are quoted separately.
